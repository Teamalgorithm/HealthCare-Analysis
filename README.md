🩺 Healthcare Data Analysis

📘 Project Overview:
This project analyzes hospital admission data to uncover patterns in patient demographics, medical conditions, billing, and hospital operations.
The goal is to deliver actionable insights that support healthcare decision-making, resource optimization, and performance improvement.

🎯 Objectives:

    🔹 Identify trends in patient conditions, treatments, and outcomes
    🔹 Evaluate hospital and doctor performance
    🔹 Analyze financial and insurance patterns
    🔹 Support planning with seasonal and occupancy insights
    🔹 Present findings through interactive dashboards

🛠️ Tools & Technologies:

    🐍 Python – Data cleaning, preprocessing, and exploratory analysis
    🧮 SQL – Data extraction, transformation, and querying for insights
    📊 Excel – Pivot tables and summary reports
    📈 Power BI – Interactive dashboards and KPI visualization

👥 Stakeholders:

    🏥 Hospital Management: Cost control & resource allocation
    👨‍⚕️ Doctors: Treatment outcomes & patient profiles
    💼 Insurance Providers: Billing accuracy & claim patterns
    📊 Data Analysts: Insight generation & strategic reporting


📂 Dataset Overview:

    🧍 Patient demographics: Name, Age, Gender, Blood Type
    💊 Medical details: Condition, Medication, Test Results
    🏥 Admission data: Admission & Discharge Dates, Doctor, Hospital
    💰 Financial details: Billing Amount, Insurance Provider, Admission Type, Room Number


📊 Data Analysts: Insight generation & strategic reporting

🔍 Key Analysis Areas:

🧍 Patient & Condition Insights:
    🔹 Most common diseases and affected age groups
    🔹 Gender distribution across conditions
    🔹 Average stay duration per condition
    🔹 Most prescribed medications and test outcomes

🏥 Hospital & Doctor Performance:
    🔹 Hospitals with highest patient intake
    🔹 Average billing amount per hospital
    🔹 Doctor performance based on patient volume or outcomes
    🔹 Admission type trends (Emergency / Elective / Urgent)

💰 Financial & Insurance Analysis:
    🔹 Average treatment cost per disease
    🔹 Comparison of insurance providers by clients & cost
    🔹 Billing trends by month and year

📊 Administrative Insights:
    🔹 Room occupancy rates and efficiency
    🔹 Seasonal admission and discharge trends
    🔹 Year-over-year case volume analysis
